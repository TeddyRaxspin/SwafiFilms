ABOUT US:

MMPK is an organization for Muslim journalists and practitioners in the media industry based in Kenya,registered and recognized by the Media Council of Kenya.The association is guided by the principle of serving the society through positive stories in the media.Besides general reporting, we consider stories that touch on basic issues affecting the Muslim Community within media, part of our social responsibility.

SERVICES:

Providing guidance to Islamic leadership on media coverage.

Consulting on Journalism, Public Relations.

Endorsing key people in the Muslim Community for media engagements.

Mentoring young journalists/interns.
